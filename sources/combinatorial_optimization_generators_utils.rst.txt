Combinatorial Optimization Generators tools
===========================================
Generators used to solve combinatorial problems could return a parsed result. This parsed result
is a class containing:

 - the raw :class:`~qat.core.Result` found by the execution
 - the raw best :class:`~qat.core.wrappers.result.Sample`
 - a parsed version of the best :class:`~qat.core.wrappers.result.Sample`, that could be displayed

.. autoclass:: qat.opt.results.GraphPartitioningResult
   :members: display

K-Clique result
--------------
Generator :class:`~qat.opt.generators.KCliqueGenerator` will cast the :class:`~qat.core.Result` returned by the QPU
into a :class:`~qat.opt.results.KCliqueResult` (which is a subclass of :class:`~qat.opt.results.GraphPartitioningResult`)

.. autoclass:: qat.opt.results.KCliqueResult
   :members: clique

Vertex cover result
-------------------
Generator :class:`~qat.opt.generators.VertexCoverGenerator` will cast the :class:`~qat.core.Result` returned by the QPU
into a :class:`~qat.opt.results.VertexCoverResult` (which is a subclass of :class:`~qat.opt.results.GraphPartitioningResult`)

.. autoclass:: qat.opt.results.VertexCoverResult
   :members: cover
