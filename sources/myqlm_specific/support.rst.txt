Support
=======

If you have any question concerning myQLM, if you need help, or if you found a
bug in this module, you can:

 - Join the `myQLM slack group <https://myqlmworkspace.slack.com>`_ to ask
   your question or help the community
 - Create a ticket on our
   `GitHub page <https://github.com/myQLM/myqlm-issues/issues>`_ to report a bug.
   Please try to make your bug reproducible by providing, if possible, a sample of
   code
